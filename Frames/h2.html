Mohandas College of Engineering and Technology(MCET)is and Engineering and Technology College located at th village Anad, Thiruvananthapuram district,kerala,India.the College,which is affiliated to the KTU, was established in 2002.Mohandas College is a project of The mohandas group of companies and is managed by the VN Gangadhara Panikker Memorial Trust.The College has received ISO 9001-2008 certification.
